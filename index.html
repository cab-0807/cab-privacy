Privacy Policy — CAB Authenticator

Last updated: April 27, 2026

CAB Authenticator is a two-factor authentication app for CAB platform administrators. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Data We Collect

  • Administrator credentials: your username and password are sent over an encrypted HTTPS connection to authenticate you. Your password is never stored on the device.
  • Authentication token: a short-lived JWT issued by the CAB server after login. Stored locally on your device using the OS secure keychain (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore) via Expo SecureStore.
  • Display name: your first and last name as returned by the server, stored locally in the secure keychain for display purposes only.

2. Device Features We Access

  • Camera: used only when you choose to scan a QR code from the admin console. No images or video are recorded, stored, or transmitted.
  • Face ID / biometric authentication: used to lock and unlock the app after a period of inactivity. Biometric data never leaves your device — it is processed entirely by iOS/Android system APIs. We have no access to it.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate you and display your one-time login code.
  • To verify QR-code scan confirmations with the CAB server.
  • To protect your session using device-level biometrics.

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any purpose other than operating the app.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party. The only external communication is with the CAB backend server (admin-auth-production-febd.up.railway.app) over HTTPS, solely to authenticate your session and deliver your login code.

5. Data Retention

  • Your authentication token and display name are stored on-device until you log out, at which point they are permanently deleted from the secure keychain.
  • No data is retained on our servers beyond what is necessary for active session management.

6. Security

All network communication uses HTTPS/TLS. Credentials are stored using the platform's secure keychain (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore). The app enforces a device passcode requirement and locks automatically after 5 minutes of background inactivity.

7. Children's Privacy

CAB Authenticator is intended for use by authorized CAB platform administrators only. It is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
